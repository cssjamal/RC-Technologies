About RC Technologies

Your Trusted Security Partner in Karachi Since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, RC Technologies began with a simple mission: to provide reliable and affordable security solutions to the people of Karachi.

What started as a small shop in Gulshan-e-Iqbal has now grown into one of Karachi's most trusted CCTV and security solution providers. We specialize in Dahua and Hikvision products, offering complete installation and maintenance services.

Our team of certified technicians has completed over 500+ installations across Karachi, from residential homes to large commercial establishments.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge security solutions that are accessible, reliable, and backed by exceptional customer service. We aim to make Karachi safer, one property at a time.

Our Vision

To become Karachi's leading security solutions provider, known for innovation, quality products, and unmatched after-sales support.
